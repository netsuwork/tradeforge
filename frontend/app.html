Forex Basics

What Is Forex?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the global market where currencies are traded.

Traders buy and sell currencies to profit from price movements.

Major Currency Pairs

  • EUR/USD
  • GBP/USD
  • USD/JPY
  • USD/CHF

What Is A Pip?

A pip is the smallest price movement in forex pairs.
