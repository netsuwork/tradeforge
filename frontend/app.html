⚠ Risk above 2% is dangerous for beginners. Professional traders use 0.5–1%.
The 1% Rule: Risking 1% per trade on a $10,000 account = $100 risk. To blow your account, you'd need 100 consecutive losing trades. That's virtually impossible with any real system. At 10% risk, just 10 losses end your career. Professional traders survive by staying in the game.

Economic Calendar: High-impact events move the forex market by 50–200+ pips in seconds. NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls), FOMC rate decisions, and CPI releases are the most powerful. Always check this before placing a trade. Red = high impact. Never trade blind into a red event.

📅 LIVE ECONOMIC CALENDAR

Real-time · All major currencies

🔴 HIGH IMPACT

NFP — Non-Farm Payrolls (USD, monthly)
FOMC — Fed interest rate decision
CPI — Consumer Price Index (inflation)
GDP — Gross Domestic Product
Central Bank — BoE, ECB, BoJ rate decisions

📋 HOW TO USE

1. Check calendar before market open
2. Note all red events for the day
3. Avoid trading 15 min before/after high-impact events
4. Watch for "Actual vs Forecast" — the difference moves the market
5. Better than expected = currency strengthens
